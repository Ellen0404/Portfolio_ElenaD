About me

I’m happy you’re reading this section- my digital introducing. I’m Russian Jewish who got married in Italy and now living in the United States) I have lived in 3 different countries, dived into 3 totally different cultures, and tried to get along with 3 different languages. Not easy, but always interesting and challenging! I got my Bachelor degree in Finance and worked a lot for financial institutions, opened my own business and tutored Hebrew. However, I always knew there is something else for me, something more artistic and creative, more fascinating. When I got my first camera I had no doubt – this is it! Freeze the moment before it fades, stop that emotion you have here and now – that’s my goal – to give you these energies, so you can relive them forever! It’s not just the passion for photography that moves me, it’s actually the people whom I photograph, their connections between each other , their feelings. How suddenly she smiles when she looks at him , how calm he becomes when he hugs her..It’s always been amazing to me how our eyes, lips, and body change when we look at the person we love! I want to show it to you, it’s so beautiful..
